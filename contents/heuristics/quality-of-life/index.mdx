# Quality of Life

This category covers heuristics that enhance the overall user experience, making interfaces more convenient, efficient, and pleasant to use.

## Why this matters

Quality of life improvements:

- Reduce cognitive load and user frustration
- Make tasks faster and more efficient to complete
- Accommodate different user preferences and contexts
- Prevent common errors and help users recover gracefully
- Build trust and satisfaction with your product

These heuristics go beyond basic accessibility to create genuinely delightful experiences.
