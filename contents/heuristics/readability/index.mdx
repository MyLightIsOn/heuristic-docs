# Readability

This category covers heuristics that ensure text content is easy to read and understand for all users.

## Why this matters

Readable content:
- Serves users with low vision, dyslexia, or cognitive disabilities
- Reduces eye strain for all users
- Improves comprehension and retention
- Works better on different devices and screen sizes
- Makes content accessible in various lighting conditions

Good readability is essential for effective communication.
